Prints the list, but sorted (ascending sort)

>>> my_list = MyList([5, 2, 8, 1, 9])
>>> my_list.print_sorted()
1 2 5 8 9

>>> my_list = MyList([9, 7, 3, 6, 2])
>>> my_list.print_sorted()
2 3 6 7 9

>>> my_list = MyList([1, 1, 1, 1, 1])
>>> my_list.print_sorted()
1 1 1 1 1

>>> my_list = MyList([])
>>> my_list.print_sorted()
